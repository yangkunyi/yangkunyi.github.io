## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Global College, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Yutong Ban. I am also part of [Sirius Lab](https://sirius.sjtu.edu.cn/), where I work on computer vision for surgical video analysis. My recent research focuses on surgical scene segmentation, vision foundation models, and AI-assisted surgical quality assessment, with an emphasis on data-efficient and clinically useful methods for real surgical environments.

## Research Interests

- Surgical video analysis
- Surgical scene segmentation
- Medical computer vision
- Vision foundation models
- AI for surgical quality assessment
