## About Me

I am affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I work on computer vision for surgical video analysis. My recent work focuses on surgical scene segmentation, vision foundation models, and AI-assisted surgical quality assessment. I am interested in building data-efficient and clinically useful methods that operate robustly in real surgical environments.

## Research Interests

- Surgical video analysis
- Surgical scene segmentation
- Medical computer vision
- Vision foundation models
- AI for surgical quality assessment
